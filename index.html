Who am I?

Hi, I'm Daniela, the creator of Samay Breathing.

Breathwork is more than just a practice for me—it's a path to self-discovery, healing, and deep connection with ancestral wisdom. In a world full of distractions, our breath is a powerful tool that brings us back to ourselves, to our truth.

My journey with breath has transformed me in ways I can't fully put into words. It has strengthened my intuition, deepened my awareness, and opened doors to experiences beyond explanation.

This website is just the beginning—what I truly want to share goes far beyond these pages. It’s about reconnecting with something ancient and sacred, something that has always been within us.
